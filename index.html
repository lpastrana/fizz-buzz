FIZZ BUZZ

In this game of FizzBuzz we are taking numbers that are divisible by 3, 5 or both. When the 'Start' button is selected you will be prompted to enter a number and the program will be executed in sequence from one until it reaches the inputted number. If the number is divisible by 3 it will state 'Fizz', by 5 'Buzz' or 'FizzBuzz if it is divisible by both.
